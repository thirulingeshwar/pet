About Our Pet Hospital

Founded in 2010, Paws & Tails Veterinary Hospital has been serving the community with compassionate, high-quality veterinary care for over a decade. Our mission is to enhance the lives of pets and their families through exceptional medical care, education, and personalized service.

Our 10,000 square foot facility is equipped with the latest technology including digital radiography, ultrasound, in-house laboratory, and a fully equipped surgical suite. We maintain the highest standards of cleanliness and safety to ensure your pet's comfort and well-being.

What sets us apart is our Fear-Free certified approach, minimizing stress and anxiety for your pet during visits. We take the time to listen to your concerns and explain treatment options thoroughly.
